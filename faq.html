Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF file safe and secure?
Yes, completely. All processing happens in your browser on your device. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere. Once you close your browser, nothing remains. Your data is 100% private.
How long does it take to convert a PDF?
Conversion time depends on file size and DPI setting. Most documents convert in 10-30 seconds. Larger files or higher DPI settings may take 1-3 minutes.
What DPI should I use?
300 DPI is recommended for most documents and balances quality and processing time. Use 600 DPI for important documents like contracts or certificates. Use 150 DPI for faster processing of draft documents.
Can I convert multiple PDFs at once?
The tool converts one PDF at a time. After converting your first file, click "Convert Another" to reset and process additional PDFs.
Will the converted file have a watermark?
No, there are no watermarks, logos, or hidden marks. The converted PDF is clean and professional with only the scanned effect applied.
What is the file size limit?
Files up to 50MB can be converted. Larger files may cause browser performance issues. Most documents are under 10MB.
Does this work on mobile devices?
Yes, the tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. However, very large files may be slower on mobile devices due to less processing power.
