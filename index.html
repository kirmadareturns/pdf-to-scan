How to Convert PDF to Scanned Document

Step 1: Upload Your PDF File

Click the upload zone or drag and drop your PDF file. Our tool accepts PDF files of any size up to 50MB. The conversion process is fast and secure‚Äîyour files never leave your device.

Step 2: Select DPI Quality

Choose your preferred DPI (resolution) setting. For most documents, 300 DPI is recommended. Higher DPI (400-600) gives better quality but takes longer to process. Lower DPI (100-200) is faster but may reduce quality.

Step 3: Click Convert

Hit the "Convert to Scanned PDF" button and wait for the process to complete. You'll see a progress bar showing real-time conversion status. The tool applies realistic scanned document effects including paper texture and noise.

Step 4: Download Your Scanned PDF

Once conversion is complete, click "Download Scanned PDF" to save your file. Your PDF now looks like a professionally scanned document with authentic scanned appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF file safe and secure?
Yes, completely. All processing happens in your browser on your device. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere. Once you close your browser, nothing remains.
How long does it take to convert a PDF?
Conversion time depends on file size and DPI setting. Most documents convert in 10-30 seconds. Larger files or higher DPI settings may take 1-3 minutes.
What DPI should I use?
300 DPI is recommended for most documents and balances quality and processing time. Use 600 DPI for important documents like contracts. Use 150 DPI for faster processing of draft documents.
Can I convert multiple PDFs at once?
The tool converts one PDF at a time. After converting your first file, click "Convert Another File" to reset and process additional PDFs.
Will the converted file have a watermark?
No, there are no watermarks, logos, or hidden marks. The converted PDF is clean and professional with only the scanned effect applied.
What file size limit is there?
Files up to 50MB can be converted. Larger files may cause browser performance issues. Most documents are under 10MB.
Does this work on mobile devices?
Yes, the tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. However, very large files may be slower on mobile devices due to less processing power.
Can I undo the conversion?
The conversion creates a new file‚Äîyour original PDF is never modified. Keep your original file if you need it later.
