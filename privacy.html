Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Overview

PDF to Scanned ("we," "us," or "our") operates the pdftoscan.online website. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

PDF to Scanned processes files only in your browser. We do not collect or store:

  • Your PDF files
  • Personal information like name or email
  • Converted PDF files
  • File contents or metadata

We may collect basic analytics data such as page views and general usage patterns (not tied to any individual) to improve our service.

3. How We Use Information

Any data collected is used only to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Monitor site security and prevent abuse
  • Generate anonymized usage statistics

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and security. These services operate under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies on their websites.

5. Browser Processing

All PDF conversion happens locally in your browser using JavaScript libraries. Your files never leave your device. When you close your browser, all data is cleared.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for site functionality and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

8. Security

We take security seriously. Since files are processed locally in your browser, they are protected by your device's security. We use HTTPS to protect data in transit.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
